## Replit Build Spec (1-page site)

### Tech

* Next.js (App Router) + Tailwind
  *(or plain HTML + Tailwind CDN if you want zero setup)*
* Single page: `/`
* Mobile-first, dark mode only

### Assets

* Use provided logo (place top-left and centered in hero as needed)
* Favicon: same logo, small

---

## Brand + UI Rules

* Background: near-black graphite
* Panels: slightly lighter graphite
* Text: soft off-white
* Accent: muted gold (matches logo)

**No gradients, no neon, no “AI sparkles.”**
This is structural + premium.

---

## Page Structure

### 1) Top Nav (minimal)

Left: Logo + “LoadFrame Systems”
Right: Button “Participate” → scroll to form

---

### 2) Hero (above the fold)

**Headline:**
Reduce revenue fragility and platform dependence.

**Subheadline (1–2 lines):**
LoadFrame stress-tests monetized creator businesses to identify structural risk across revenue concentration, platform dependency, and offer architecture.

**Primary CTA:** “Participate in the Study” (scroll to form)
**Secondary CTA:** “See what you’ll get” (scroll to deliverables)

**Micro-cred line (small):**
For monetized creators earning ~$5k–$50k/month.

---

### 3) “What We Measure” (3 cards)

Card 1: **Revenue Concentration**
One source carrying most of the weight = single point of failure.

Card 2: **Platform Dependency**
If reach drops, does income collapse with it?

Card 3: **Offer Architecture**
Do you have a stack—or one overloaded beam?

---

### 4) Deliverables (what participants receive)

**Selected participants receive:**

* A visual **Load Map** of your business
* A **Fragility Index** score
* 3 structural risk findings + next moves

*(Keep it simple—no overpromising.)*

---

### 5) “Who This Is For” (2 columns)

**Good fit**

* Monetized creators (ads/sponsors/products/services)
* High-ticket operators / course sellers
* AI-era solopreneurs with fragile funnels

**Not a fit**

* Pre-revenue beginners
* Pure hobby channels
* Anyone wanting “growth hacks”

---

### 6) The Form (embedded on page)

Use a clean card panel.

**Fields (keep short):**

* Name
* Email
* Primary platform (YouTube/IG/TikTok/Other)
* Follower/subscriber range (dropdown)
* Monthly revenue range (dropdown)
* Revenue breakdown % (Ads, Sponsors, Products/Services, Affiliates, Other) *(can be 5 number inputs)*
* Biggest worry if reach drops 50% (short answer)
* “Open to a 15-min call?” (Yes/No)

Button: **Submit**

After submit: “Thanks — if selected, you’ll receive an email within 7 days.”

---

### 7) Footer

Small. Quiet.

* “© LoadFrame Systems”
* Contact email (once you set it up)

---

## Copy Tone Rules

* Calm, engineered, non-hype
* Avoid: “10x, viral, crush it, AI-powered”
* Use: “stress-test, structural, fragility, concentration, dependency”
